How to Recycle Properly

Recycling can sometimes be tricky, but following these simple steps will help ensure that your recyclables are processed correctly:

Step 1: Know What Can Be Recycled

Different items require different recycling methods. Be sure to check the recycling symbols on packaging and follow local guidelines to see what can and can't be recycled.

Step 2: Clean and Sort Your Items

Before you recycle, rinse containers to remove food waste. Sorting recyclables by material type (e.g., paper, plastic, metal) also helps improve efficiency in recycling facilities.

Step 3: Avoid Contaminating Recyclables

Do not place non-recyclable items like plastic bags, electronics, or hazardous waste in your recycling bin. This can ruin an entire batch of recyclables!

Step 4: Use Recycling Centers

Always use local recycling centers or curbside collection to ensure your recyclables are properly processed. Some items may require special handling, such as e-waste or batteries.
